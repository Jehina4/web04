About Me

This is an informative website about the wildlife, marine life and its conservation methods in Mauritius.

I am Jehina Arjoon and I'm currently a university student in Mauritius at University Technology of Mauritius (UTM), and this website is part of a project I was assigned—one that quickly became a personal mission. I've combined my love for design, storytelling, and marine life into a digital space that’s both educational and creative, its my way of sharing the beauty of our island’s biodiversity while learning and growing as a developer.
