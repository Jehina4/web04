Wildlife in Mauritius

Mauritius is not only about sun, beaches ,sand and resorts, it also has its wildlife. The wildlife in Mauritius consist of fauna and flora. However it has a high number
of endemic and endangered species of mammals, bird, reptiles and plants. Due to the isolation of Mauritius, it has a low range of wildlife. Now most of these widlife
are threatened with extinction due to human activities including habitat destruction
and appearance of the non-native.

Marine life in Mauritius

Mauritius provides a wide variety of marine life which attracts tourist to visit our beautiful island. it consists of different types of fishes, corals and other sea creatures which are detailed in this website.

Conservation methods

Conservation efforts in Mauritius is based in the protection of endangered species and their habitat. The ways of conservation of wildlife and marine life are provided in this website.
